## üìß Alternative: LinkedIn PDF Export

LinkedIn allows you to download your profile:

1. Go to **Me** ‚Üí **Settings & Privacy**
2. Click **Data Privacy**
3. Click **Get a copy of your data**
4. Select what you want to download
5. Click **Request archive**
6. Wait for email with download link

*(Note: This only works if you're logged in as Alina)*

## üí° Tips for Better Extraction

1. **Take screenshots** - Visual backup of everything
2. **Use browser "Save as PDF"** - Print entire profile to PDF
3. **Copy to a document** - Paste everything into a Word/Google Doc first
4. **Check "Show all X experiences"** - Click to expand hidden entries
5. **Look for "See more"** - Many sections are collapsed
6. **Check Activity tab** - Recent posts and articles
7. **View posts individually** - Some might have more content
